## Missouri Security Framework

### 19 CSR 100-3.010 — Premises Security
- Camera coverage: all entry/exit, all storage, all sales, all cultivation, all processing, all loading/unloading
- **90-day minimum footage retention**
- Visitor log: name, date, time, purpose, escort
- Security plan on file with DCR; updated within 30 days of material change

### 19 CSR 100-3.020 — Alarm Systems
- 24/7 central monitoring
- Panic buttons at POS and cash-handling
- Motion detection in storage and cultivation

---

## The Medical-Era Security System Problem

Missouri's medical marijuana operators installed security systems in 2018–2021 under the medical program's requirements. Many of those systems were configured for the medical program's retention standard — which was lower than the adult-use program's 90-day requirement. When adult-use launched February 2023, operators who did not specifically upgrade their security systems for adult-use compliance were immediately out of compliance on retention.

**The 2026 reality:** Some of these systems are now 5–7 years old. Hard drives, camera components, and alarm system hardware are at or past end-of-life. A security system failure discovered during a DCR inspection is a corrective action, not a maintenance notification.

## Storage Capacity Math

Same calculation as Ohio/Michigan:
- 20 cameras at 1080p, 24/7 = approximately 45 TB minimum for 90 days
- Standard 8–16 TB NVR = 30–40 days maximum at this resolution
- **The gap is real and measurable — run the math for your facility**

## Compliance Hardening Checklist — Missouri Security

- [ ] Medical-era security systems audited for 90-day retention compliance
- [ ] DVR/NVR storage capacity calculated for 90 days at current camera count + resolution
- [ ] Hard drive age verified — systems >4 years should have drives replaced
- [ ] Footage retrieval from Day 85 tested and confirmed
- [ ] Camera coverage audit including any post-launch facility modifications
- [ ] Security plan filed with DCR; updated within 30 days of material changes
- [ ] Alarm monitoring contract current and active monitoring verified
- [ ] Panic buttons at all POS + cash handling
- [ ] Visitor log — digital preferred, all required fields
