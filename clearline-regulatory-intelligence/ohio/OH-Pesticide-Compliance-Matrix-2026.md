## Section D: ODA Applicator License Verification

**Ohio Requirement (ORC § 921.02):** Commercial pesticide applications must be made by or under supervision of an ODA-licensed Commercial Pesticide Applicator.

| Party | Required ODA Credential | How to Verify |
|---|---|---|
| In-house IPM staff applying pesticides | ODA Commercial Pesticide Applicator license | ODA online license lookup |
| IPM contractor | ODA Pesticide Business License + applicator credential | ODA online + request copy |
| Out-of-state contractors | Ohio reciprocity / Ohio license required | Verify with ODA; reciprocity not automatic |
