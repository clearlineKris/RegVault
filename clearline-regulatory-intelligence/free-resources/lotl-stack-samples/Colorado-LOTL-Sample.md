# Colorado LOTL Stack Sample (Excerpt)

This sample shows how the LOTL (Letter of the Law) format is structured for Colorado. It highlights the first portion of the statute-to-checklist conversion used in the full product.

## Excerpted Sections (first 5 pages equivalent)
1. **Licensing Obligations**
   - CRS 44-10-301: Retail Marijuana Establishment Licensing — *Pinpoint*: (2)(a)-(f)
   - Required documents checklist and renewal timelines.
2. **Seed-to-Sale Tracking**
   - MED Rule 3-105: Tracking System Participation — *Pinpoint*: (B)(1)-(4)
   - Device, access, and reconciliation requirements.
3. **Security & Access Control**
   - MED Rule 3-225: Premises Security — *Pinpoint*: (A)-(C)
   - Camera coverage, retention, and visitor controls.
4. **Packaging & Labeling**
   - CRS 44-10-203 & MED Rule 3-1010 — *Pinpoint*: THC symbols, warning statements, child-resistant standards.
5. **Waste Handling**
   - MED Rule 3-1505 — *Pinpoint*: rendering unusable, manifests, and transport.
